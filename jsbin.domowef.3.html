FRIED RICE RECIPE 2 👌👌🔥🔥

I enjoy fried rice for its versatility and comforting flavors. It's a delicious way to use leftover rice and can be customized with different vegetables, proteins, and seasonings. The combination of savory soy sauce, tender rice, and varied textures is always satisfying.🥰🥰🥰

Ingredients ✅ ✅

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 table spoon vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs slightly beaten
  • 2 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions 💯💯

  1. Prepare ingredients
    • Ensure the rice is cooked.
    • Dice the onion, mince the garlic and prepare the mixed vegetables🧄🧄
  2. Cooking fried rice
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet
    • Add the beaten eggs to the pan and scramble until cooked.🥚🥚
  3. Stir fry vegetables
    • In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil
    • Add the diced onion and minced garlic and stir for about 1-2 minutes
    • Add mixed vegetables to the pan and cook for another 1-2 Minutes until they are tender and crispy
  4. Combine rice and flavors
  5. Seasoning
    • Drizzle soy sauce and oyster sauce over the rice🥫🥫
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste🌶️🌶️🔥
  6. Final touches
    • Stir in the scrambled eggs that were set aside earlier 🍳🍳
    • For added flavors, you can drizzle a little sesame oil over the fried rice at the end
  7. Serve
    • Garnish with chopped green onions or scallions 🍛🍛
    • Serve hot as main dish or as a side with other dishes 🍲🍲

Tips ✅ ✅

  • Use Day-Old Rice: Freshly cooked rice tends to be too moist and sticky for fried rice. Using day-old rice allows it to dry out slightly, resulting in better texture.
  • High Heat: Stir-frying is best done over high heat to keep the ingredients crispy and prevent them from becoming soggy.
  • Customization: Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables and proteins. This dish is versatile and can accommodate various ingredients based on your preference.

The completed fried rice should have a vibrant appearance with distinct grains of rice coated in a savory golden-brown hue. It should emit a delightful aroma of garlic, soy sauce, and toasted sesame oil. Each bite should deliver a perfect balance of flavors—umami from soy sauce, sweetness from vegetables, and a hint of richness from eggs or protein, creating a satisfying and aromatic dish.
